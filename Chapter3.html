Chapter 3

One more year passes. The man comes home from work to find his house on fire again. The man sees the little green man once again at the scene of the crime. He begins to chase the little green man and the little green man gets in his car. The man gets in his car and continues to chase the little green man


The little green man drives to the airport and gets on a plane. The man gets on the plane then lands at the airport and chases the little green man again. The little green man gets in his taxi. The man gets in his taxi and chases the little green mans taxi. They both get out of their taxi's and the man sees the little green man get on his boat.


But this time the man is even more prepared and has a boat of his own. He chases the little green man on his boat to this small uninhabited Island where he see the little green man get off. He looks around the island but cannot find the little green man. He searches every tree, bush and behind every rock. He then see the little green man just sitting on a beach fishing. He goes over picks up the little green man and scream "Did you burn down my house!". The little green man looks back at him and says...."no". The man replies "Oh okay then, sorry for the mistake" and leaves.
