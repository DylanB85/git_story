Chapter 4

The man realising his mistake, apologised to the little green man and started to make his way home. He left the island on the boat that he originally took. He then travelled to he gets a taxi back to the airport. He takes the plane back to his hometown filled with guilt.

The man lands in his hometown. He gets in his car and drives back home. He again finds his house on fire wondering why is this happening? He sees the little green man again he goes to chase him and he runs away. Too tired to even try anymore the man just lets him away this time.
